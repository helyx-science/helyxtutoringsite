Helyx Tutoring Program

Helyx is excited to tutor students all over the world in various subjects. Please click on an intended subject below to register for the mailing list. Click here to join meetings for each class.

Mathematics

With our experienced math tutors, students will learn analytical skills and concepts, including geometry, number theory, and probability, to help them succeed in math competitions, including MathCounts and AMC8. Additionally, students learn the basic concepts of math including but not limited to manipulating simple equations, determining probabilities, and interpreting graphical data. Click to get started!

Computer Science

With long term experienced programmers with experience at top industries, students will learn how to program in multiple languages for multiple disciplines, ranging from machine learning to web development. Additionally, students get to take away mini projects from each session that look good on resumes. Click to get started!

Biology

Designed for aspiring biologists, students will learn about advanced biology concepts, with a focus on genetics, evolution, and biological processes within and between organisms. Click to get started!

Chemistry

With our experienced national chemistry olympiad participants and outstanding students in AP Chemistry as tutors, Students will learn a wide range of chemistry concepts ranging from basic to AP level, including atoms, periodic trends, chemical reactions, bonding, and more. Click to get started!

Physics

With our experienced physics tutors, students will gain a fundamental understanding of the laws governing the natural world, learning analytical, conceptual as well as intuitive skills in kinematics, dynamics, energy, and electricity, to prepare for high-school level algebra-based physics curriculum. Click to get started!
